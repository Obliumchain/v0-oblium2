### Image Specifications
- **Format**: PNG (recommended for transparency) or JPG
- **Size**: 512x512 pixels or 256x256 pixels (square aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Keep under 200KB per image for optimal loading
- **Style**: Should match your OBLIUM brand aesthetic (metallic, geometric, futuristic)

### How to Upload
1. Navigate to the `public/avatars/` folder in your project
2. Delete or replace the existing placeholder images
3. Upload your 10 custom avatar images with the exact filenames above
4. The avatar selector will automatically use your new images

### Design Recommendations
- Use consistent style across all 10 avatars
- Consider metallic silver/chrome colors to match your logo
- Include cyan/blue accents for brand consistency
- Ensure avatars are easily distinguishable from each other
- Make sure they look good at small sizes (displayed at 80x80px on mobile)

### Testing
After uploading, visit the Profile page to verify:
- All 10 avatars display correctly
- Clicking an avatar selects it
- First-time selection awards 1,000 points
- Selected avatar shows on the leaderboard
